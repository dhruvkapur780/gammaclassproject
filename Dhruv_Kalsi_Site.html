01. About

Driven by a passion for finance and an obsession with precision, I am a finance and accounting student at the Kelley School of Business focused on investment banking. I am drawn to the high-stakes, pressure-filled nature of the industry. My academic foundation in financial analysis and accounting has equipped me with the tools to tackle complex economic problems—I'm excited to apply these in roles that demand rigor, resilience, and adaptability.

The precision that defines motorsports mirrors the approach I bring to my career.

About

Hi! I’m Diya, a freshman at Indiana University’s Kelley School of Business. I am deeply interested in financial services and curious about how finance, data, and technology come together to influence real business decisions. I enjoy understanding how things work and approach problems with an analytical mindset, especially when there is a clear question to solve and meaningful impact to create.

As I continue my time at Kelley, I am focused on building a strong foundation as an analytical, data-driven, and collaborative student while staying open to new experiences, challenges, and opportunities.

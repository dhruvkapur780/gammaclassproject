About Me

I am a sophomore at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business pursuing a double major in Information Systems and Operations Management with a co major in Business Analytics and a minor in Merchandising. I am passionate about the intersection of data and retail, and I am especially interested in how analytics can inform merchandising strategy, optimize operations, and elevate the customer experience. Through coursework and leadership roles, including serving as a K201 Peer Tutor and Logistics Chair for a 300 plus attendee campus event, I have developed strong technical skills in Excel, Access, and Power BI while learning how to translate data into clear, actionable decisions.

Outside of academics, I enjoy watching gymnastics and keeping up with fashion and shopping. I'm always curious about how current trends shape what we see in retail. Beyond my degree requirements, I'm also deepening my analytics skills through additional study in Power BI.

About Me

I'm a freshman at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, pursuing a double major in Finance and Computer Science. Originally from Aurora, Illinois, I graduated from Metea Valley High School with a 4.3/4.0 GPA and earned AP Scholar with Distinction.

As a Coding Tutor at iCode School, I taught K–8 students programming languages like Java and HTML, 2D/3D digital art, and CAD and 3D printing. I'm a DECA National Qualifier (3rd place in Sports & Entertainment Marketing) and former state qualifier. I speak English, Telugu (fluent), and French (elementary), and I'm focused on exploring where finance meets technology.

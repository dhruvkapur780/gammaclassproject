// 04 Research

Published
Work

Published Paper

Forecasting Cryptocurrency Price Movement Using Transformer-Based Deep Learning & Stance Detection

Cryptocurrency markets are notoriously volatile and inaccessible to everyday investors. Existing sentiment analysis approaches label posts based solely on English language signals. This research uses NLP-driven stance detection with respect to a specific crypto entity — combined with transformer-based deep learning models — to predict price movement more accurately. The goal: make crypto investment insight accessible to everyone.
